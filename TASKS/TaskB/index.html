My Old Kentucky Home, written by: Stephen Foster

The sun shines bright on my old Kentucky home, 'Tis summer, the people are gay; The corn-top's ripe and the meadow's in the bloom While the birds make music all the day. The young folks roll on the little cabin floor, All merry, all happy and bright; By 'n' by hard times comes a knockin at the door, Then My Old Kentucky Home, good night!
